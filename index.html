What is TDS?

TDS (Trip Data Standard) is an open, lightweight format for sharing anonymised trip-level data in a standardised way across micromobility operators. Based on common industry practices like GBFS and MDS, TDS allows operators to easily publish a trips.json feed for secure ingestion into the central CIE-managed database.

Why TDS?

Public bodies, transport planners, researchers, and industry stakeholders need a clear view of how people are moving through cities. TDS provides a simple and privacy-compliant way to gather real-world usage data from micromobility systems across Europe. Insights generated from the data help improve infrastructure, guide policy, and strengthen the future of shared transport.
